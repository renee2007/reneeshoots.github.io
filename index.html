05 — About

Hi, I'm Reneesa.

Photography has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up, my dad was always the one behind the camera, capturing the smallest moments and turning them into memories. I think that's where it all began for me. I didn't realize it at the time, but I was slowly learning to see the world the way he did.

I started taking photos at a young age, but it only became something real to me in 8th grade, when I got my first camera. That's when I began to understand that photography wasn't just about pictures. It was about noticing things. Light falling in a certain way, quiet moments, little details that most people overlook.

I've never really been good with words. I don't always know how to explain what I feel, and maybe that's why I hold on to photography so tightly. It gives me a way to express things I can't say out loud. It lets me turn feelings into something I can keep.

I'm drawn to aesthetics in a way I can't fully explain. Soft light, still moments, the kind of scenes that feel calm but emotional at the same time. I like capturing things the way they felt in that exact moment, not just the way they looked.

To me, photography is not just about taking pictures. It's about holding on to something before it disappears.
